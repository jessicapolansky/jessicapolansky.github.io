Guide to Hero.RPG

JessicaRobinson -- Est. 03/15/18

Overview

Hero.RPG is a text-based game where you kill monsters to obtain gold. The gold can then be used to purchase weapon upgrades or potions at the town shop.

Game Play

This game is based on encounters and multiple-choice decision making. As you encounter new creatures, you can choose to:

  1. Fight!
  2. Stand very still.
  3. Bravely flee.
  4. Head to the shop.

Hero Types

There are currently 2 types of hero that you can choose to play. Each has their own unique abilities that make gameplay interesting.

Warrior

The warrior is your most basic hero type. He can do basic attacks with 5 power and has a starting 10 health. However, upon attacking, there is a ~20% chance the attack will cause 2x damage.

Medic

The medic is similar to your most basic hero type, starting with 5 power and 10 health. In addition to the basic attacks, there is a ~20% chance for a 'bandaid' to be used that heals the medic for 2 points. A current glitch in the code actually allows you to heal above full health!
